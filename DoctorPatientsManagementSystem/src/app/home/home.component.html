Doctor Patient Management

The Doctor-Patient Management System app provides a secure platform for patients to store and access their medical records. Patients can upload and manage their health information, including previous diagnoses, allergies, medications, lab reports, and vaccination records. This feature ensures that doctors have comprehensive and up-to-date information about their patients' medical history, leading to more accurate diagnoses and personalized treatments.
